Echoes of the Ancients

Serena Nightingale

Echoes of the Ancients" by Serena Nightingale is a gripping fantasy novel set in a world where ancient powers and forgotten magic hold sway. When young scholar Lysandra discovers an ancient artifact, she unwittingly sets off a chain of events that could reshape the very fabric of her world. As dark forces rise and ancient prophecies awaken, Lysandra must embark on a perilous journey to unlock the secrets of the past and prevent a catastrophe that threatens to plunge the realm into chaos. With allies both old and new, she delves into the mysteries of lost civilizations, battling foes both mundane and magical, in a quest that will test her courage, wisdom, and resolve.
